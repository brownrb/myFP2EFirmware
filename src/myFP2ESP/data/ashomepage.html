This device is a myFP2ESP WiFi controller, and allows connection via USB Serial, Bluetooth, TCP/IP, Webserver, and ASCOM REMOTE. The controller supports OLED display and Temperature Probe and controls a stepper motor which is attached to the focuser shaft of a telescope, permitting remote control of the focuser.
